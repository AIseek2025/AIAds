## Executive Summary

This document outlines the strategic roadmap for AIAds platform over the next 12 months following the successful 100% full launch. The roadmap focuses on scaling the business, expanding features, and establishing market leadership in the micro-KOL marketing space.

### Vision Statement

**Become the leading micro-KOL marketing platform for Chinese businesses going global, connecting 1 million KOLs with 20,000 advertisers by May 2027.**

### Strategic Pillars

1. **User Growth** - Scale to 1M+ KOLs, 20K+ advertisers
2. **Business Expansion** - Achieve $15M monthly revenue
3. **Technical Excellence** - 99.99% availability, <80ms P95
4. **Market Leadership** - 30% market share in micro-KOL segment

## 6. Risk Management

### 6.1 Key Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| **Competitor Response** | High | High | Speed to market, network effects |
| **Platform Policy Changes** | Medium | High | Multi-platform, direct relationships |
| **Economic Downturn** | Medium | High | Diversified verticals, cost efficiency |
| **Talent Shortage** | High | Medium | Remote hiring, training programs |
| **Technical Debt** | Medium | Medium | Dedicated refactoring sprints |
| **Regulatory Changes** | Medium | High | Legal team, compliance first |

### 6.2 Contingency Plans

**If growth slows:**
- Increase marketing spend efficiency
- Focus on retention over acquisition
- Accelerate international expansion
- Consider strategic partnerships

**If funding delayed:**
- Extend runway to 18 months
- Prioritize revenue-generating features
- Reduce non-essential hiring
- Focus on core markets

**If technical issues:**
- Dedicated reliability team
- Increased testing coverage
- Slower rollout cadence
- Enhanced monitoring
